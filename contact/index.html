Frequently Asked Questions

How do I become a member?

Membership is open to all Columbia University alumni with connections to Africa. Learn more about membership or contact us directly.

Can I contribute to the blog?

Yes! We welcome blog contributions from our community. Visit our blog page to submit an article or email us your ideas.

How do I join the mentorship program?

Our mentorship program connects experienced alumni with recent graduates. Learn more about the program or contact our mentorship team.

Do you organize events?

Yes! We host regular networking events, workshops, and panel discussions. Check our events calendar or join our Discord for announcements.

How can my organization partner with CU-AA?

We're always interested in partnerships that benefit our community. Contact our partnerships team to discuss opportunities.

Where can I find job opportunities?

Our members share exclusive job postings and collaboration opportunities. Access the opportunities board (member login required).
